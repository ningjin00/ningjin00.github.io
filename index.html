About me

Jin Ning received the B.S. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2014, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from the University of Electronic Science and Technology, Chengdu, China, in 2020. She is currently a Lecturer at Chengdu University of Technology. Her current research interests include computer vision, remote sensing, and data mining.

宁 进 于2014年获得中国成都电子科技大学学士学位，于2020年获得电子科技大学计算机科学与技术博士学位。现为成都理工大学讲师。她目前的研究兴趣包括计算机视觉、遥感和数据挖掘。她平均一年6篇高质量论文
